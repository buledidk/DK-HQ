# audit-planning-materiality

The planning + materiality phase, to FRC / PCAOB file-review standard.

## Overall audit strategy (ISA 300 ¶7)

- Scope: reporting framework, industry, laws/regs
- Timing: interim / year-end / subsequent events / completion / reporting
- Direction: team, specialists, supervision
- Preliminary engagement activities: ISQM 1 acceptance/continuance,
  independence, engagement letter
- Knowledge obtained feeds risk assessment (ISA 315)

## Audit plan (ISA 300 ¶9)

Per significant area: nature/timing/extent of risk-assessment procedures;
nature/timing/extent of further procedures (ISA 330); team + hours; review plan.

## Materiality framework (ISA 320)

### Overall Materiality (OM)
Benchmark × percentage. Select benchmark on users' needs + stability.

| Benchmark | Range | When |
|-----------|-------|------|
| PBT | 5% (listed), 5–10% (private) | Profit-stable |
| Revenue | 0.5–1% | Loss-making / growth |
| Total assets | 0.5–2% | Asset-heavy (property, funds) |
| Net assets | 1–5% | NFP, investment entities |
| Total expenditure | 1–3% | Charities |

Adjust (document): normalise one-offs; 3-yr average if volatile; down if
regulator scrutiny high.

### Performance Materiality (PM)
PM = OM × 50–75%. 50% higher risk; 75% low risk.

### Clearly-Trivial Threshold (CTT)
CTT = OM × 5% (cap 3%). Below CTT disregarded (ISA 450).

### Specific Materiality
Lower threshold for: director remuneration, related-party, CASS, covenants,
regulatory minima, sensitive KPIs.

### Component Materiality (groups)
≤ Group materiality; see `group-audit`.

## Practice

- Initial at planning (latest available + extrapolated)
- Revise when draft FS differ materially
- Revise (usually downward) if new risks emerge
- Document every change + rationale

## Strategy memo structure

1. Engagement overview
2. Regulatory + reporting framework
3. Scope (group, components)
4. Materiality (OM, PM, CTT, Specific, Component)
5. Significant risks
6. Overall responses (controls vs substantive; specialists)
7. Resources + timeline
8. Communication plan (TCWG, mgmt, regulator, component auditors)
9. Approvals (EP, EQR, manager)

## Anti-patterns

- Benchmark chosen to maximise materiality
- PM = OM (no aggregation buffer)
- Materiality not updated when FS change
- No specific materiality for director remuneration / related-party / regulated
- Materiality memo not signed off before fieldwork
