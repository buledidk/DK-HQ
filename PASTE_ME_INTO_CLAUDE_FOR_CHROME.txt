- If the raw URL returns 404, log "MISSING <name>" and continue.
- If you hit a login prompt, CAPTCHA, or the save button returns an error,
  pause and ask me what to do — don't retry silently.
- Do NOT modify, summarise, or re-format the description or instructions.
  Paste verbatim.
