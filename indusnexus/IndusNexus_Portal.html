The Semiconductor Opportunity

🏭 Global Semiconductor Market

The global semiconductor market is projected to reach $1 trillion by 2030, driven by AI, data centres, EVs and 5G infrastructure. Supply chain disruptions and geopolitical shifts create extraordinary trading opportunities for agile, intelligence-led firms.

$1T Market by 2030 · CAGR 8.6% ↗

🤖 AI Chip Demand Surge

NVIDIA, AMD, Intel and TSMC's order backlogs signal sustained high-value chip demand through 2026–2028. Indus Nexus positions at the intersection of supply intelligence and procurement agility to capture premium margins in constrained chip categories.

NVIDIA H100/H200 · AI Accelerators · GPU Trading ↗

🔗 API Economy Growth

The global API management market is growing at 25%+ CAGR, with trading, fintech and supply chain integration as the fastest verticals. Firms with strong API integration capability command significant premium as the connective layer of modern commerce.

API Market: $13.2B → $148B by 2030 ↗

🌍 Cross-Continental Trade Corridors

Post-CHIPS Act and EU Chips Act, semiconductor trade flows are being restructured. New corridors between Europe, South Asia, SE Asia and the Americas are opening, creating first-mover advantage for London-based intermediaries with multi-continental intelligence.

CHIPS Act · EU Chips Act · Friend-Shoring ↗
